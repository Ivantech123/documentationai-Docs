## Get Started in Minutes

Set up your Андреев Иван account and launch your first documentation space quickly. Follow these steps to register, create a space, explore the dashboard, and add your initial project page. You will be documenting in under five minutes.

<Callout kind="info">
**Prerequisites:**
- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)
- An email address for registration
- Optional: Google account for quick sign-in
</Callout>

## Create Your Account

Sign up using your preferred method. Андреев Иван supports email registration or social login.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Email" icon="mail">

    1. Visit `https://dashboard.example.com/register`.

    2. Enter your email and create a strong password.

    3. Verify your email via the confirmation link.

  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Google" icon="apple">

    1. Click "Sign in with Google" on the login page.

    2. Authorize the app in your Google account.

    3. Access your dashboard immediately.

  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Create Your First Documentation Space

Once logged in, set up a space for your project docs.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose Space Type" icon="package">
    Select "New Documentation Space" from the dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name Your Space" icon="edit-3">
    Enter a name like "My Project Docs" and add a description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize Settings" icon="settings">
    Set brand color to `#3B82F6` and invite team members if needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch Space" icon="rocket">
    Click "Create Space" to generate your docs home.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Navigate the Dashboard

The dashboard provides quick access to your spaces. Key sections include:

- **Spaces List**: View and manage all documentation spaces.
- **Recent Pages**: Jump to your latest edits.
- **Templates**: Start with pre-built docs structures.

| Section       | Purpose                          |
|---------------|----------------------------------|
| Spaces        | Manage documentation collections |
| Pages         | Edit and organize content        |
| Settings      | Configure branding and users     |
| Analytics     | Track page views and engagement  |

Use the search bar to find pages across spaces.

## Add Your Initial Project Page

Create a page inside your space for project details.

<Steps>
  <Step title="New Page" icon="plus">
    Click "New Page" in your space sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Content" icon="edit">
    Use the MDX editor to write markdown with components.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview and Publish" icon="eye">
    Preview changes, then hit "Publish".
  </Step>
</Steps>

Here is an example MDX code block for your first page:

<CodeGroup tabs="Markdown,With Components">
```markdown
# Welcome

This is your project home.
```

</CodeGroup>
