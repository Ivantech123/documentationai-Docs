## Start Organizing

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a project">
    Sign in at `https://andreev-ivan.com` and click "New Project".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add documentation">
    Click "Add Document" and enter your content or upload files.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite collaborators">
    Use the "Invite" button in your project to add team members.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share docs">
    Set permissions and send shareable links as needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>
